Asteroids

Instructions

Smash into the blue asteroids for points. Try to get as many points as you can. Avoid hitting the black and green UFOs—if you hit them, you lose the game.

Keys: W/A/S/D or Arrow Keys (↑, ←, ↓, →). You can also move diagonally by pressing two keys together (e.g., W + A = up-left). Hold keys down to move continuously.
